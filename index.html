Throughout this online portfolio, you will find a variety of materials that illustrate my teaching style and philosophy, including lesson plans, learning assessment samples, and reflections on my own teaching practice. I hope that by sharing these materials, you will gain a better understanding of how I strive to create a dynamic and engaging learning experience for my students. The purpose of this portfolio is to showcase my teaching philosophy, approach, and experiences in the classroom. The title of my portfolio, "Inspire", reflects my core belief that as educators, our ultimate goal is to inspire our students to not only learn, but to think critically, creatively, and compassionately about the world around them.
